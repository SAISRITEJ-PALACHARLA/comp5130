Ice Cream

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Cooking Time: 3 hours (including freezing time)

Instructions: Whisk the cream, milk, and sugar together. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn for 30 minutes. Freeze for 2-3 hours before serving.

Vegetarian

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • Spices: coriander, cumin, chili powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions: Marinate paneer in yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Skewer the paneer and grill until golden brown. Serve with chutney or naan.

Vegetable Curry

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 cup peas
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • Spices: cumin, turmeric, chili powder

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Instructions: Fry onions and cumin. Add tomatoes and spices, and cook until the sauce thickens. Add vegetables and simmer for 15 minutes. Serve hot with rice.

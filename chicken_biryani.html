Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 500g Basmati Rice
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 3 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Spices: Cardamom, Cloves, Cinnamon
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Fresh Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt.
  2. Fry onions and tomatoes until golden brown.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a separate pot, cook rice until half-done.
  5. Layer rice and chicken, then steam on low heat for 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
